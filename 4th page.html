What is Dogecoin?

Dogecoin is a cryptocurrency created by software engineersBilly Markus and Jackson Palmer, who decided to create a payment system as a joke, making fun of the wild speculation in cryptocurrencies at the time. Despite its satirical nature,some consider it a legitimate investment prospect.
